What is Lawn Care Tracker?

Lawn Care Tracker is a free, research-backed lawn care program designed to help DIY homeowners maintain healthy, beautiful lawns using science-based guidance from university extension services.

Unlike commercial lawn care apps that promote specific products, our programs are built entirely from peer-reviewed research conducted by agricultural universities across the United States.
